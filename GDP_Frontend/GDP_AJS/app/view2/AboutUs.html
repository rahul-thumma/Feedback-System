Online Feedback System for student presentations:

Feedback System invites international colleges and students to use Feedback system as an online service to provide and take feedback about the presentations that are given in their respective colleges
Faculty can assign topics to students and can form groups. Students will give presentations based on their topics. Presentations can be given either individually or with a group. Students can give live feedback on other presentations.
Student will have facilities like updating their profile, providing feedback for others, history of their activities etc.
Admin will have rights to create classes, add and remove students to different classes, allocate faculty to classes. Faculty will have rights to assign topics to the students and almost all other controls over students that an admin generally have.
